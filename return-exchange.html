Return Policy

Eligibility

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with original tags attached
  • Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" are not eligible for return or exchange

Process

  1. Fill out the return/exchange form above
  2. Receive confirmation and return shipping label via email
  3. Pack the item(s) securely with all original packaging
  4. Schedule a free pickup or drop off at nearest courier partner
  5. Refund/exchange will be processed within 5-7 business days of receiving the return

Refund Options

  • Original payment method refund
  • Store credit (additional 5% bonus)
  • Bank transfer (for COD orders)
